1. Introduction

Welcome to Habit Pro, a habit tracking application developed by Smart IT Services. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Habit Pro is a fully offline, local-first application that respects your privacy by design.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

Habit Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User account credentials or login information
  • Habit data, goals, or tracking information
  • Device identifiers or unique IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

3. How Your Data is Stored

All data you create in Habit Pro is stored locally on your device using the device's secure storage mechanisms:

  • Local Storage Only: Your habits, goals, streaks, and all tracking data remain exclusively on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not offer cloud synchronization or backup services
  • Complete Control: You have full control over your data - it never leaves your device
  • App Deletion: If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features (PIN, password, biometric authentication)
  • We do not have access to your data at any time
  • There is no risk of data breaches from our servers because we don't maintain any servers for user data
  • Your data cannot be compromised through internet transmission because the app works offline

5. Permissions

Habit Pro may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage: To save your habit data locally on your device
  • Notifications: To send you habit reminders (optional, can be disabled)

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve any data collection or transmission.

6. Third-Party Services

Habit Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics or tracking tools
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

7. Children's Privacy

Habit Pro is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns for children using the app. Parents can allow their children to use Habit Pro with confidence.

8. Data Backup and Export

Users are responsible for backing up their own data if desired. We recommend:

  • Using your device's built-in backup features (iCloud for iOS, Google Drive for Android)
  • These backups are managed by Apple/Google and subject to their respective privacy policies
  • Habit Pro does not have access to these backups

9. App Updates

When you update Habit Pro through the App Store or Google Play Store:

  • Update information is handled by Apple/Google, not by us
  • We do not track who updates the app or when
  • Your local data remains unchanged and private after updates

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes that affect how we handle data (though we currently don't collect any), we will:

  • Update this page with the new policy
  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Notify users through an in-app message

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

11. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no data for us to access, correct, or delete
  • You maintain complete control over your data on your device
  • You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings

12. International Users

Habit Pro can be used by anyone worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect any information, there are no international data transfer concerns. The app complies with privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other international privacy laws by design (through not collecting data).
